How it works

- Every tree is given a random number upon creation. (From 1-1000000)

- This number (seed) is sent to you and used to create a list of 100000 numbers.

- The numbers are used to create a unique and replicable tree, drawn on your screen every time you visit.

- Over approx. 20 days your tree will gradually increase in size, growing every 10 minutes.

- Watering can be done once per day. Each time slightly increasing the growth rate.
